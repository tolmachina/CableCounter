Count your cables for d&b audio arrays

Upload your array.dbea and cablerun.csv files


Create a .csv file with points of your cable run to the array. Each point must be on a new line in a format x, y, z coordinates in meters. Points are relavent to the plane in your ArrayCalc project Firts point must be the position of amps. For example:

0,3,0

0,10,0

2,10,0

2,10,6

Would describe a cable run from amps at x=0, y=3 and z=0. Cable run goes 7 meters side of stage, until next junction, then 2 meters forward, then gets to the array and goes 6 meters up in "z" plane to reach speakers.

Export .dbea file from ArrayCalc. Right click on your array and choose export.
